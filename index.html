In-Game Rules for Star Wars Roleplaying

1. No random killing or team killing allowed.

2. Respect the lore and stay in character.

3. No metagaming or using out-of-character information in the game.

4. Follow the chain of command in roleplay scenarios.

5. Use appropriate language and behavior in the in-game chat.

6. Do not engage in power-emoting or overpowering other players.

7. Avoid disrupting roleplay events and follow event guidelines.

8. Check our forums for announcements, events, and community discussions.

9. Report any rule violations to our moderators for a fair and enjoyable experience.

10. Have fun, respect each other, and may the Force be with you!

Additional Information

Welcome to Imperial Military | RP, a dynamic Star Wars universe on Roblox where you can immerse yourself in epic roleplaying adventures.

Explore the galaxy, build alliances, and create your own story within our community of passionate Star Wars fans.

Gameplay Features:

  • Engage in intense roleplaying scenarios with a dedicated community.
  • Participate in exciting missions and challenges that unfold within the Star Wars universe.
  • Experience a rich and immersive environment with attention to Star Wars lore.
  • Customize your character, gear, and spacecraft to enhance your roleplaying experience.

Community Activities:

  • Join events and gatherings hosted by our community managers and moderators.
  • Connect with fellow players on our Discord server for real-time communication and updates.
  • Participate in forum discussions to share your experiences, suggestions, and engage with the community.
  • Stay updated with announcements, upcoming events, and community news on our official platforms.

Whether you're new to Star Wars roleplaying or a seasoned adventurer, Imperial Military | RP offers a welcoming space for all enthusiasts.
